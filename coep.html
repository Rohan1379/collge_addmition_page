About: COEP is one of India's oldest engineering colleges, known for innovation, academic excellence, and student startups. Key Points about COEP: Legacy and Recognition: Established in 1854, COEP is one of Asia's oldest engineering institutions and was granted "Unitary Technological Public University" status in 2022, operating as COEP Technological University. It is ranked 77th in the engineering category by NIRF 2024. 1.Academic Infrastructure: 2.The campus features modern departmental labs, extensive library resources (over 120,000 books, journals, and digital resources), and provides access to online databases and e-books. Autonomous Curriculum: 3.As a university, COEP now has a fully autonomous curriculum design, allowing for greater flexibility and interdisciplinary options compared to its previous university-regulated system. Placements and Industry Links: 4.COEP boasts a strong placement record, with a dedicated placement cell facilitating 100% placements for many students in reputed companies, offering competitive salary packages. It also provides internship opportunities to a large percentage of students. Research and Innovation: 5.The university emphasizes research and innovation, with extensive research autonomy and a dedicated Research and Innovation Park established in partnership with the Government of Maharashtra. This park aims to foster industry-academia collaborations and develop prototypes through joint projects.
